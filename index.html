Common questions

What our clients ask
before they sign on.

How do I know Adora can actually deliver results?

Fair question. We are deliberate about the clients we take on — we only work with businesses where paid ads are genuinely the right channel, which we determine in the free audit. That selectivity is why we offer a reduced first-month rate: so you can see the work before committing long-term. We earn the relationship.

What is the return if ads do not convert straight away?

Paid ads rarely hit their ceiling in the first two weeks. The first month is calibration — testing audiences, refining creative, cutting what is draining budget. By month two, cost-per-lead typically drops 20–40% as the campaign learns. We walk you through realistic timelines in the audit so there are no surprises.

I've tried ads before and got burned.

That experience is more common than it should be — and the cause is almost always the same. Bad results are a strategy problem, not a platform problem. Meta and Google work. Bring your previous campaign data to the audit and we will diagnose exactly what failed and how we would approach it differently.

How long before I see results?

Paid ads aren't a switch you flip — they compound. The first couple of weeks are about building audience data and letting the platform learn. By week three or four, the system starts finding the right people at the right cost. We tell you this upfront because we'd rather set honest expectations than overpromise a result we can't guarantee on day one.
